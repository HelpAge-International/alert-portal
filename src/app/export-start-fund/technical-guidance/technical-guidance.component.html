FREQUENTLY ASK QUESTIONS
What should I name this document?

Please save a new copy of the file with the following format: "Start Fund Project Proposal - 'your agency name', 'crisis name' Response.


Will I be able to change the proposal after submission?

The submitted proposal is treated as a final document, however if you recieve a conditional award, you will need to change the proposal to meet those conditions and then resubmit to the Start Team (startfund@startnetwork.org)

 Who do I need to send this proposal to and when?

Please respond by the date stated in the accompanying email and return the form to: startfund@startnetwork.org


Who should I contact if I have issues with the form?

For questions related to deadlines and submission, please contact the Start Fund team at: startfund@startnetwork.org

For M&E related questions contact the Start Fund MEAL team at: david.burt@worldvision.org.uk

The Start Fund 1% Learning Budget

The Start Fund sets aside 1% of its total funding for learning. Start Network member agencies can further strengthen humanitarian response through learning from experience and increased involvement of communities. Agencies can apply for this funding (up to Â£10,000 per Alert) during or after any Start Fund project they are awarded. The application is short and any activity can be proposed with any (suggestions are in the guidance) timeframe and budget they wish. Once an application is received, the Start Fund MEAL team will reply with a decision at the next scheduled selection date.
